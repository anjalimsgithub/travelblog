Andaman & Nicobar Island

Comprises 572 islands that lie at the juncture of the Bay of Bengal and the Andaman Sea.

Andaman & Nicobar Island

The Andaman group has 325 islands while the Nicobar group has only 24 islands. North, middle and South Andaman are collectively known as the Great Andaman.

Andaman & Nicobar Island

The Andaman group is separated from the Nicobar group by a 150 km wide channel known as the Ten Degree Channel.

Top Places to visit in Andaman Trip

ROSS ISLAND

Ross Island is another place that will leave you spellbound. This island was the erstwhile capital of Andaman until 1941 when an earthquake destroyed the island. Ross Island was the headquarters of the Indian Penal Settlement for nearly 80 years.The island had markets, bakery, stores, workshop, water distillation plant, church, tennis court, printing press, secretariat, hospital, cemetery, open air theatre and others. Today, everything has disappeared except some old dilapidated buildings, which housed some of these landmarks.

PORT BLAIR

Port Blair is the capital of Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Also the largest town, it is also the entry point for all the other islands. So in all probability your trip will start from here. There are a few places of interest in the town like the Cellular Jail, Samudrika Marine Museum, and water sports complex among others.
